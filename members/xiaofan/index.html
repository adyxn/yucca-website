Research Interests

  • Edge AI and energy-efficient computing
  • Embedded system design for real-world cyber-physical applications
  • Neuro-symbolic computing for continual (lifelong) learning tasks

I am broadly interested in building intelligent and autonomous embedded systems. Much of my work involves collaborations with academia and industry partners (full collaboration list).

  • designing and conducting research schedules,
